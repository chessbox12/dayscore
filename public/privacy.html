DayScore Privacy Policy

Effective 21 July 2026

DayScore (the website and the Chrome extension) does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There is nothing to opt out of, because nothing is gathered in the first place.

Where your data lives

Everything you enter — daily ratings, optional notes, settings, and the mini-game high score — is saved in your browser's local storage, on your device. It never leaves your device. There is no server, no account, no sync, and the extension makes no network requests.

What the extension can access

The extension requests no browser permissions. It cannot read your tabs, browsing history, or anything outside its own popup. It uses no analytics, no cookies, and no third-party services.

Deleting your data

You can erase everything at any time from the app's settings, by removing the extension, or by clearing your browser's site data. Once cleared, the data is gone — we never had a copy.

Changes & contact

Any future changes to this policy will be posted at this address. Questions or concerns? Open an issue at github.com/chessbox12/dayscore/issues.
